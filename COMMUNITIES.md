## Post-Quantum Cryptography Communities

### NIST Post-Quantum Cryptography Project
- **Website**: https://csrc.nist.gov/projects/post-quantum-cryptography
- **Focus**: Standardization of post-quantum cryptographic algorithms
- **Opportunity**: Participate in standardization discussions, submit implementations
- **Timeline**: Ongoing standardization process

### PQC Forum
- **Website**: https://groups.google.com/a/list.nist.gov/g/pqc-forum
- **Focus**: Discussion group for post-quantum cryptography
- **Opportunity**: Share research findings, engage with researchers
- **Format**: Mailing list and discussion forum

### ETSI Quantum-Safe Cryptography
- **Website**: https://www.etsi.org/technologies/quantum-safe-cryptography
- **Focus**: European standards for quantum-safe cryptography
- **Opportunity**: Contribute to European standardization efforts
- **Format**: Technical committees and working groups

### Open Quantum Safe (OQS) Project
- **Website**: https://openquantumsafe.org/
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/open-quantum-safe
- **Focus**: Open-source implementations of quantum-resistant cryptography
- **Opportunity**: Contribute code, participate in community discussions
- **Alignment**: High - our LibOQS.jl component aligns with this project

## General Cryptography Communities

### International Association for Cryptologic Research (IACR)
- **Website**: https://www.iacr.org/
- **Conferences**: CRYPTO, EUROCRYPT, ASIACRYPT, TCC, PKC, FSE
- **eprint Archive**: https://eprint.iacr.org/
- **Opportunity**: Publish papers, present at flagship conferences
- **Deadlines**: Vary by conference (typically Feb-May for fall conferences)

### Cryptology ePrint Archive
- **Website**: https://eprint.iacr.org/
- **Focus**: Rapid publication of cryptography research
- **Opportunity**: Preprint publication with quick turnaround
- **Process**: Open submission, instant publication

### Real World Crypto Symposium
- **Website**: https://rwc.iacr.org/
- **Focus**: Bridging theory and practice in cryptography
- **Opportunity**: Present practical implementations and benchmarks
- **Format**: Annual conference (typically January)

### Lattice Coding and Crypto Meeting
- **Website**: https://www.latticemeeting.org/
- **Focus**: Lattice-based cryptography and coding theory
- **Opportunity**: Specialized venue for lattice research
- **Format**: Annual workshop

## Open Science & Preprint Archives

### arXiv
- **Website**: https://arxiv.org/
- **Categories**: cs.CR (Cryptography), cs.DS (Data Structures and Algorithms), math.NT (Number Theory)
- **Opportunity**: Immediate open access publication of research
- **Process**: Free submission, moderated review
- **Timeline**: 1-2 day turnaround for publication

### IACR Communications in Cryptology
- **Website**: https://cic.iacr.org/
- **Focus**: Open access cryptography journal
- **Opportunity**: Peer-reviewed open access publication
- **Format**: Diamond open access (free for authors and readers)

### Journal of Cryptology (IACR)
- **Website**: https://www.springer.com/journal/145
- **Focus**: Premier cryptography research journal
- **Opportunity**: High-impact peer-reviewed publication
- **Format**: Traditional journal with IACR affiliation

### Journal of Mathematical Cryptology
- **Website**: https://www.degruyter.com/journal/key/jmc/html
- **Focus**: Mathematical aspects of cryptography
- **Opportunity**: Publish mathematical foundations and analysis
- **Format**: Peer-reviewed journal

## Notes

- **Open Access**: Prioritize open access venues (arXiv, IACR eprint) for maximum visibility
- **Reproducibility**: Use Zenodo or OSF to archive code and data with DOIs
- **Community Engagement**: Regular updates on forums and social media build credibility
- **Dual Publishing**: Combine preprints (fast) with peer-reviewed venues (credibility)
- **Julia Ecosystem**: Leverage Julia community's focus on scientific computing
- **Standards Participation**: Engage with NIST and IETF for real-world impact
